About

About the Portfolio

This portfolio showcases a semester-long exploration of AI's creative and analytical capabilities, highlighting projects that span from reimagining poetry and exploring literary patterns to generating visuals, crafting interactive experiences, and critically engaging with digital literacy. Each experiment reflects the transformative possibilities of AI in art, storytelling, and cultural analysis.

About the Author

Matthew Bryan is a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Writing and Rhetoric at the University of Central Florida, where he specializes in rhetoric, composition, and digital pedagogy. With a Ph.D. in progress in Texts and Technology and an M.F.A. in Creative Writing, his work explores the intersections of AI, writing studies, and multimodal communication. He is also an experienced writing program administrator, researcher, and advocate for innovative approaches to teaching and learning.
